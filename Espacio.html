Espacio Bidding

" Going Once, Going Twice, Sold!! "

Overview

The world of electronic interests you? Grab this opportunity to exploit the skills in you. Indulge yourselves in a series of mind bending challenges along with modelling designs relevant to space systems .Hold on! Here is a twist, the participants have to deploy their bidding skills to catch hold of the right components to design their models. The participants get to design their module using the coolest and compact kit: PRAYOGeek- Electronics “Hands On”, Anytime, Anywhere. About kit: Click Here

Details

  • Registration Fee: ₹ 150 per team
  • Prize Money: 1st - 2000 INR ; 2nd - 1000 INR
    Both the winners get to use PRAYOGeek for period of 3 months to do Projects.
  • Date: 16-09-2018

    • ROUND-1:

    This round has some interesting challenges. A series of some mind boggling puzzles will be given to the participants. using the point earned in this round the participants get to bid the components in the next round.

    ROUND-2:

    One space system will be decided prior to the event. Auction will be on the electronic components required to build the different sub modules of that space system. Sub modules will be assigned to each teams through a small game.Then participants have to buy the components required to complete model assigned to them.So this round tests the basic knowledge of electronics in space systems.

    ROUND-3:

    In this round participants will design their respective module using PRAYOGeek kit and test their module with various test cases.
    Note: Volunteer will help the the team to use the PRAYOGeek kit.
